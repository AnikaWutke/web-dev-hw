About the Author

Shel Silverstein was born in Chicago. He is well known for his book The Giving Tree, although he had a hard time getting it published. He also wrote numerous funny poems and books of poems including A Light in the Attic from which Zebra Question originates. He wore many hats as a creator and has credits as a cartoonist, songwriter, poet, playwrite, and author. He died of a heart attack at age 68.
