Fudge Brownies

Ingredients

  • One box Betty Crocker Fudge Brownie Mix
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Toppings of Choice
    • vanilla ice cream
    • caramel sauce

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine brownie mix, water, vegetable oil, and eggs
  2. Mix until well blended and there are no lumps of dry ingredients
  3. Pour mixture evenly into a baking pan. I use a glass one because it works best
  4. Bake at 350o for 27 minutes, or until they smell done and a toothpick comes out clean
