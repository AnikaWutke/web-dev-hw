About

I am a Media Artist based in Montana. I have had a love of film since I was a little girl, so that is where my main area of expertise lies. My passion has always been storytelling and I have loved learning new ways to tell stories. I have a BFA in Media Arts: Filmmaking from the University of Montana where I got to work with animation, narrative film, and documentary.
